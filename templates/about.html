Smart Hostel and Mess Management System

About Project

Roomify is a smart student accommodation and mess management platform designed to simplify the process of finding and managing rooms and mess services. The system connects students, room owners, and mess providers on a single digital platform. Students can search rooms, apply online, view locations, and manage mess subscriptions easily. Room and mess owners can manage listings, hosted students, requests, and payment records efficiently. The platform also includes document verification, WhatsApp reminders, online payment support, and AI-based sentiment analysis for reviews. Roomify improves transparency, security, and communication between all users through a modern and user-friendly interface.

Objectives

  • Develop a single platform for students and owners
  • Enable online room booking and mess subscriptions
  • Provide secure student verification
  • Track payments and hosted students digitally
  • Use AI-based sentiment analysis for reviews

Key Features

🏠 Room Booking

Students can search and apply for rooms online.

🍽 Mess Subscription

Students can subscribe to mess services digitally.

💳 Payment Tracking

Track online and offline payments efficiently.

🤖 AI Sentiment Analysis

Analyze reviews as positive, neutral, or negative.

📱 WhatsApp Reminder

Owners can send instant payment reminders.

🔒 Secure Verification

Aadhaar and College ID verification for safety.
